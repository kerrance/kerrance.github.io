Join me as I show you how to make five of my favourite classic cocktails!

You can watch the video on YouTube.

Recipes

Gin & Tonic

  • 2 shots of your favourite gin
  • 200ml tonic water
  • raspberries as garnish

Dark 'n' Stormy

  • 1 shot of spiced rum
  • half a squeezed fresh lime
  • 200ml ginger ale
  • lime as garnish

Mojito

  • handful of mint sprigs
  • half a fresh lime
  • 2 teaspoons of granulated sugar
  • 2 shots of white rum
  • sparkling/soda water
  • mint as garnish

Mai Tai

  • 1 shot spiced rum
  • 1 shot white rum
  • 1 shot cointreau or triple sec
  • half a squeezed fresh lime
  • 1 shot orange juice
  • 2 teaspoons simple syrup
  • 1 teaspoon granulated sugar
  • You can also add a shot of grenadine for extra sweetness, but I didn't have any when I made this!
  • orange as garnish

Pina Colada

  • 2 shots coconut rum
  • 2 shots coconut milk
  • 150ml pineapple juice
  • pineapple as garnish
